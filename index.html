Space Ace

Space Ace is an up-and-coming game by Reagan Scott. Play as Cecelia, a spunky mechanic on the space ship "Dawn's Light" as she and the rest of the crew explores the cosmos! Led by their captain Eleanore Genti, Cecelia works alongside her sister B, a gentle giant by the name of Fabio, and an increasingly curious AI known as 604-2a. Not everything is peaceful in outer space, though. There are rumors of beasts the size of stars, and the farther away from Earth's colonies explorers wander, the less likely they are to return home... In a game where choices you make will affect the outcome, it's up to you to negotiate with mysterious aliens, save your sister from herself, and... Teach a robot to love?

Features:

  • Multiple endings!
  • Optional romance sidestory!
  • All original art and music!
  • Diverse characters!

Although still in very early development, this game will eventually become available on itch.io and possibly even steam. Be sure to check back regularly in order to catch the release!
